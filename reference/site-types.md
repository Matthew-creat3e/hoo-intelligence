# Site Types — Reference (Layer 3)
**Canonical source. Load only the industry type that matches the client.**

---

## 1. LOCAL SERVICE
*Contractors, handymen, landscapers, plumbers, pressure washing*

- **Sections:** Hero → Services → About → Testimonials → Pricing → CTA
- **Tone:** Tough, reliable, no-nonsense blue collar
- **CTA:** "Get a Free Estimate" / "Call Now"
- **Trust signals:** Years in business, jobs completed, service area, licenses, insurance
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue, Oswald, Barlow Condensed

---

## 2. CANNABIS / DISPENSARY
*Dispensaries, delivery services — like TCB Collections*

- **Sections:** Age Gate → Hero → Strain Grid → Deals → About → Location → CTA
- **Tone:** Chill, premium, knowledgeable, lifestyle
- **CTA:** "Shop Now" / "View Menu"
- **Trust signals:** Lab tested, locally grown, licensed, legal disclaimer visible
- **Fonts:** Rajdhani, Oswald, Bebas Neue
- **Compliance:** Age gate every page load, no medical claims, lab-tested badge

---

## 3. FAITH-BASED BRAND
*Faith streetwear, Christian merchandise — like NoReturn Apparel*

- **Sections:** Hero → Collections → Scripture Callout → About → Testimonials → CTA
- **Tone:** Bold, purposeful, spiritual authority
- **CTA:** "Shop the Collection" / "Join the Movement"
- **Trust signals:** Mission statement, community, scripture integration, founder story
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue, Playfair Display, Montserrat

---

## 4. E-COMMERCE STORE
*General product stores, online retail*

- **Sections:** Hero → Featured Products → Collections Grid → About → Reviews → CTA
- **Tone:** Matches brand identity
- **CTA:** "Shop Now" / "Browse Collections"
- **Trust signals:** Reviews, shipping info, return policy, secure checkout badges
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue, Barlow Condensed, Anton

---

## 5. RESTAURANT / FOOD SERVICE
*Restaurants, cafes, food trucks, catering*

- **Sections:** Hero → Menu → About → Hours/Location → Gallery → Order CTA
- **Tone:** Warm, inviting, appetizing
- **CTA:** "Order Online" / "Reserve a Table"
- **Trust signals:** Years open, Google/Yelp reviews, local awards, farm-to-table
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display, Oswald, Bebas Neue

---

## 6. HEALTH & WELLNESS
*Spas, gyms, personal trainers, practitioners, chiropractors*

- **Sections:** Hero → Services → About → Testimonials → Booking CTA → FAQ
- **Tone:** Calm, professional, trustworthy
- **CTA:** "Book Your Appointment" / "Start Your Journey"
- **Trust signals:** Certifications, years of experience, client results (no medical claims)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display, Raleway, Oswald

---

## 7. PROFESSIONAL SERVICES
*Law, finance, consulting, accounting, real estate*

- **Sections:** Hero → Services → About/Team → Case Studies → Testimonials → CTA
- **Tone:** Authoritative, polished, confident
- **CTA:** "Schedule a Consultation" / "Get Started"
- **Trust signals:** Credentials, case results, client logos, awards, years in practice
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display, Raleway, Oswald

---

## 8. AUTO / TRADES
*Auto detailing, mechanic, towing, body shop*

- **Sections:** Hero → Services → Gallery → About → Reviews → CTA
- **Tone:** Tough, direct, blue collar pride
- **CTA:** "Get a Quote" / "Call Now"
- **Trust signals:** ASE certified, years in business, fleet served, warranty, before/after photos
- **Fonts:** Bebas Neue, Oswald, Barlow Condensed

---

## Client Intake Interview (5 groups, one at a time, wait for answers)

**Group 1 — Business Basics**
- Business name, what they do, city/state, target customer

**Group 2 — Industry + Tone**
- Which of the 8 types above? Which tone feels right?

**Group 3 — Colors + Design**
- HOO standard (#050505 + gold) or custom hex?
- Logo file? Reference sites? Color dealbreakers?

**Group 4 — Sections + Features**
- Which sections from the master list? Any extras?

**Group 5 — Copy + Content**
- Existing tagline? Copy to use? Photos ready?
- The ONE thing that makes visitors take action?
