## Conversion Playbook

### The HOO Advantage (what to say)
1. "We build it free. You see it live. You only pay if you love it."
2. "Your competitors [NAME] have websites and get the Google traffic. You don't."
3. "One job that comes through your website pays for the whole year."
4. Reference specific competitors, specific issues, specific numbers.

### Close Process (8 steps)
1. **Find** — lead engine identifies businesses without websites
2. **Research** — verify no website, find owner name, phone, email
3. **Reach out** — email (automation) or call (Matthew's Top 5)
4. **Build free** — don't wait for yes, build a demo section/page
5. **Show** — "Here's what we built. Yours to use either way."
6. **Close** — "Want the full build? Can start this week."
7. **Deliver** — build full site, go live
8. **Retain** — monthly management, updates, support

### Objection Handling
| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I don't need a website" | "Your competitors [NAME] have one and they're getting the Google calls you're not" |
| "I can't afford it" | "One job from your website pays for the whole year. And we build it free first" |
| "I'll think about it" | "No pressure. The demo is yours either way. herrmanonlineoutlook.com when you're ready" |
| "My nephew can build one" | "Great! If they don't, we're here. Build free, pay on approval" |

## Shopify Partner Program — Hidden Revenue Stream

### What It Is
Free to join. Shopify pays HOO a recurring commission every time a client signs up through HOO's referral link and stays active. This is **passive income stacked on top of HOO's build fees** — Matthew gets paid to do what he's already doing.

### The Numbers
| Revenue Source | How It Works | Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| **Shopify referral commission** | 20% of client's monthly Shopify plan for the lifetime of that account | $6-50/mo per client referred |
| **Development store builds** | Build in a free dev store, transfer to client on launch | No cost to Matthew or client during build |
| **Theme commissions** | If HOO ever sells a theme on Shopify Theme Store | $0 now, future option |
| **Shopify Plus referrals** | Refer a Plus client ($2,000+/mo plan) | ~$400/mo recurring per client |

### Real Math for HOO
Every client Matthew signs up for Shopify through his Partner link:
- Basic plan ($39/mo) → HOO earns ~$8/mo **forever** as long as that client stays on Shopify
- 10 clients on Basic → $80/mo extra, zero work
- 25 clients → $200/mo extra on top of build fees and monthly management
- This compounds — every new client adds to the passive stack

**Combined revenue model at 25 clients:**
```
Build fees (one-time):     $7,500-11,250 (avg $400 each)
Monthly management:        $1,875-2,500/mo (avg $100/mo)
Shopify Partner commissions: $200-400/mo (avg $10/client)
TOTAL MONTHLY at 25 clients: $2,075-2,900/mo recurring
```

### How to Apply
1. Go to partners.shopify.com — free signup
2. Create a Partner account under HOO (herrmanonlineoutlook.com)
3. Get your unique referral link
4. Use that link when setting up every new client's Shopify store
5. Done — commissions start automatically

### Development Store Advantage
Once a Partner, Matthew gets **unlimited free Shopify dev stores** — fully functional stores with all features unlocked. This means:
- Build the full site before the client pays a cent
- Client sees the finished product live before any money changes hands
- "Build free, pay on approval" becomes even more powerful — the store is literally free to build and demo
- Transfer to client account only when they approve and pay

### When to Apply
**Now.** Requirements are minimal — just a business, a website (herrmanonlineoutlook.com qualifies), and agreement to Partner terms. No revenue threshold, no approval wait. Takes 15 minutes. Every day without it is leaving commission money on the table from future signups.

### Shopify Partner vs Shopify Affiliate
- **Partner** (what HOO wants) — recurring commission, dev stores, client management dashboard, full tools
- **Affiliate** (lesser version) — one-time bounty only, no dev stores, no management dashboard
Always apply as a Partner, not an Affiliate.
