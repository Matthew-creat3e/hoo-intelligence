## Industry Patterns (hypothesis — update with data)

**Tattoo shops:** Owner is usually the artist. Personal call > email. Name-drop their specific work.
**Fencing contractors:** Seasonal — spring/summer are hot. Focus on "jobs lost to competitors with websites."
**Food trucks:** Instagram/Facebook heavy. Show them what a landing page does that social can't (Google search).
**Landscaping:** Phone call converts better than email — outdoor workers check phone not inbox.
**Handyman:** High volume of calls already — angle is "stop losing the jobs you're not even hearing about."

## Scoring Calibration
HOT 50+ | WARM 30-49 | COLD <30

Best predictors of HOT score:
1. Business is established (2+ years, real reviews visible)
2. Owner name findable (Facebook, BBB, GMB)

- No social media presence at all
