## Storm Damage
**Q: How do I know if my roof has storm damage?**
A: Look for missing shingles, dented gutters, or granules in your downspouts. Most hail damage is invisible from the ground. A free professional inspection with drone and ladder access is the only way to know for sure.

**Q: Will my insurance cover storm damage to my roof?**
A: If damage is from hail, wind, or a fallen tree, yes. We handle the entire insurance process — documentation, adjuster meeting, and negotiations. 98% of our claims get approved.

**Q: How much does a new roof cost with insurance?**
A: Most homeowners pay $0 out of pocket. Insurance covers the full replacement minus your deductible. Without insurance, most Kansas City homes run $8K-$15K depending on size and material.

**Q: How long after a storm should I get my roof inspected?**
A: As soon as possible. Insurance claims have time limits. We recommend scheduling a free inspection within 30 days of any major storm event.
