## How It Works
**Q: How does online personal training work?**
A: You connect with your trainer via Zoom or FaceTime for live coaching sessions. Between sessions, custom workouts are delivered through an app with video demos. Your trainer checks in daily via text to keep you accountable.

**Q: What equipment do I need for online personal training?**
A: It depends on your goals. Many clients start with just dumbbells and a resistance band. Programs are built around whatever you have access to — even bodyweight only works.

**Q: Is online training as effective as in-person training?**
A: Yes. With live video sessions, real-time form correction, and daily accountability, online training delivers the same results. Many clients prefer the convenience of training from home on their schedule.
