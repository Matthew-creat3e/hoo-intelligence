Questions

COMMON QUESTIONS

How much does weekly mowing cost?

Most residential yards in Independence run $35–$65 per visit depending on yard size. Use our instant quote calculator above for a quick estimate, or call (816) 555-0147 and we'll give you an exact price in minutes.

Do I need to sign a contract?

Never. We don't do contracts. You stay because the work speaks for itself. Cancel anytime with a simple text or call — no fees, no hassle, no questions asked.

What happens if it rains on my mow day?

We reschedule to the next dry day at no extra charge. You'll get a text letting you know. We never mow wet grass — it's bad for your lawn and makes a mess.

Do you bring your own equipment?

Yes — commercial-grade mowers, trimmers, edgers, blowers. Everything. You don't need to provide anything. We bring it all and leave your property spotless.

Are you licensed and insured?

Fully licensed, insured, and bonded. We carry general liability and workers' comp. Your property is protected and so is our crew.

How fast can you start?

Most new clients are on the schedule within 48 hours. If we have availability, same-week starts are common. Call (816) 555-0147 or fill out the form and we'll let you know immediately.
