↳ Why Shopify (and why we won't put your business on anything else)

SECURITY · BANK-GRADE BY DEFAULT

Shopify is PCI-compliant out of the box. Every credit card transaction, every customer login, every personal detail your store collects — secured to the same standard a bank uses, and none of it touches our servers.

LONGEVITY · NOT GOING ANYWHERE

Shopify is 14 years old, public on the NYSE ($SHOP), and runs roughly 10% of every online store on earth. The platform you build on today will still be there in 2036 — and your store with it.

LESS VULNERABLE · BY A MILE

Compared to WordPress — where 94% of every CMS hack last year happened — Shopify is a closed platform. No plugin exploits. No outdated PHP. No 3am ransomware.

We could host your store on cheaper platforms. We don't, because your store's job is to make money 24/7 — not give us a side hobby of patching security holes at midnight.

You'll get two bills every month. One from HOO ($136 or $260). One from Shopify ($39, billed direct to your card). No middleman, no markup, no "trust us, we'll handle the billing."
