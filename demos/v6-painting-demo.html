06How we protect your home

The prep no one else bothers with.

You hired us to paint your house — not trash it. Here's exactly what happens before a single drop of paint goes on the wall.

01

Pre-walk + plan

10 minutes together before a single tool comes off the truck. We flag off-limits areas, confirm colors, note pets and access points. No surprises once tarps are down.

Takes10 minutes
02

Everything gets covered

Canvas drop cloths on every floor — never plastic, it slips. Furniture centered and wrapped. HVAC vents sealed so paint dust doesn't circulate. Light fixtures bagged.

FloorsCanvas · non-slip
03

Precision masking

FrogTape on every baseboard, trim piece, outlet, switch plate, and window frame. Clean lines, zero bleed-through, no sticky residue when we pull it.

TapeFrogTape Delicate
04

Repairs before paint

Nail holes filled. Drywall cracks patched and sanded flush. Rotted exterior boards flagged and replaced. Loose caulk cut out and redone. Paint can't fix what's broken.

IncludedPatch · Caulk · Replace
05

Dustless sanding

Festool DC sanders with HEPA extraction on every handheld and pole sander. Near-zero dust in your air, on your photos, or in your food. Allergies + kids safe.

ExtractionHEPA · Festool
06

Clean exit

Two premium coats applied. Tape pulled clean while paint's still tacky (no chipping, ever). Vacuum on the way out. Furniture back exactly where it started. Walkthrough before we leave.

FinalWalkthrough + warranty
Our guarantee: if any damage happens to your home during the job — a scuff on a baseboard, a drop on your floor, a scratch on your furniture — we fix it or replace it. Written into every contract. No arguments.
