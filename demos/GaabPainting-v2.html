What We Do

OUR SERVICES

INTERIOR PAINTING

Walls, ceilings, trim — clean lines and zero mess. We prep, prime, and paint every room like it's our own home.

EXTERIOR PAINTING

Curb appeal that lasts. Power wash, scrape, prime, and two coats of weather-resistant paint — built to handle Missouri seasons.

CABINET REFINISHING

New kitchen feel without the remodel price tag. Sand, prime, spray — factory-smooth finish on your existing cabinets.

DECK STAINING

Protect your deck from sun, rain, and snow. We clean, sand, and apply premium stain that keeps wood looking rich for years.

DRYWALL REPAIR

Holes, cracks, water damage — patched and blended so you'd never know it was there. Texture-matched to your existing walls.

COLOR CONSULTATION

Not sure what color? We bring swatches to your space, test them in your lighting, and help you pick something you'll love for years.
