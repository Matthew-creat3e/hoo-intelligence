Quick Answers

How much does a cleaning cost?

Deep cleans range $130-$340 by home size. Recurring saves 15-20%. Use the quote builder at the top for an instant estimate.

Do I need to be home?

Nope. Most clients give us a key or garage code. All cleaners are background-checked, bonded, and insured.

What products do you use?

Eco-friendly, non-toxic by default. Safe for kids, pets, and allergies. We can also use your preferred products.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We re-clean any areas free within 24 hours. No questions. That's our guarantee.

How do I pay?

Card, Venmo, Zelle, or cash. Payment after you're satisfied with the clean.
