FAQ

Common Questions

How does online training work?

We connect via Zoom/FaceTime for live sessions. Between sessions, workouts are in an app with video demos. I check in daily via text.

What equipment do I need?

Depends on goals. Many start with dumbbells and a band. I build around whatever you have — even bodyweight only.

How much does it cost?

Phone coaching starts at $149/mo. Virtual 1-on-1 is $249-$399/mo. First consultation is always free.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. Month-to-month. The 12-week transformation is a commitment, but standard programs cancel anytime.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?

Absolutely. Over half my clients started as beginners. I meet you where you are. That's the point of custom programming.
