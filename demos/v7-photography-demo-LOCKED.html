About — A letter from the photographer

Rowan
Hale.

Founder and photographer. Based in Kansas City. Available for weddings in the Midwest — and anywhere couples will have her.

I didn’t set out to be a wedding photographer.
I set out to be an honest one.

I grew up in small-town Missouri, around weddings held in church basements and backyards. My grandmother kept every photograph in a cedar box. When I opened it, the pictures smelled like smoke and roses — and I could tell you who loved whom, forty years later, from a single frame.

That’s what I’m after now. Not the perfect shot — the true one. The breath before the vows. The way your dad looks at you at the end of the aisle. The hand you reach for without thinking.

I shoot film first, digital second. I work with one second-shooter I trust. I deliver in two weeks, not six months. And I only take twelve weddings a year, so I can hold each one all the way through.
