Common Questions

Most KC homes run $8K-$15K. With storm damage, insurance typically covers 100% minus your deductible.

Will my insurance cover it?

If damage is from hail, wind, or fallen tree — yes. 98% of our claims get approved.

How long does replacement take?

3-5 days for most homes. We clean up every day before we leave.

What brands do you use?

GAF and Owens Corning. 25-year workmanship warranty on every roof.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. 0% financing available for qualified homeowners. Plus, most storm damage claims mean $0 out of pocket.
