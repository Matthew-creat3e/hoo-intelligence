FAQ

Real questions from real people.

How much does a local KC move cost?

A 2-bedroom local move averages $900–$1,400 with North Star. Studios start at $299. The only way to know exactly is to build your quote above — every dollar is itemized, no "call for pricing."

Do you pack my stuff for me?

Yes. Choose Pack + Move mode and our crew handles every box. We bring the boxes, the packing tape, the blankets, the bubble wrap — no charge for supplies. If you prefer to pack yourself, choose Just Move and we haul.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. USDOT 3481222. MC 894221. Licensed by the Missouri Department of Transportation since 2008. Every move is covered by our in-writing $10,000 damage guarantee — plus full commercial cargo insurance.

How far in advance should I book?

For weekends and end-of-month, 2–3 weeks. Mid-week moves can usually be booked with 3–7 days notice. We always save a few slots for last-minute emergencies — call (816) 555-0123 and ask.

What about piano / pool table / hot tub?

We move them. Uprights $180, grand pianos $350, pool tables $200, gun safes $120, hot tubs $400. Add them to your quote above — locked price includes the extra crew time and special equipment (dollies, straps, padding).

What does the quote include?

Everything. Truck, fuel, crew, dollies, blankets, shrink wrap, tape, bubble wrap, stairs, long carries, weekend surcharges — all included. If it's not on the invoice we quoted, we're not charging you.

Do you move out of state?

Yes — anywhere in the contiguous 48. Long-distance quotes are built the same way as local: everything itemized, locked in writing before we load. Call for an out-of-state quote, or build one above and we'll call back in 15 min.

Can I reschedule if plans change?

Yes, free up to 48 hours before your move. Inside 48 hours, we ask for a $99 reschedule fee only if we can't refill the slot. Life happens — we'll work with you.
