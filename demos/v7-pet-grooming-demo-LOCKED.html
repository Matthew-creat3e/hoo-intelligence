Questions

Common Questions

How much does dog grooming cost?+

Bath & brush from $35 (small), full groom from $55. All include nail trim, ears, and gland expression. Pricing varies by coat condition.

Do I need an appointment?+

Appointments recommended for full grooms. Walk-ins welcome for bath & brush based on availability. Call (816) 555-7297 or book online.

How long does grooming take?+

Bath & brush: 45-60 min. Full groom: 1.5-2.5 hours. We text you a photo when your pet is ready.

Do you groom cats?+

Yes! Bath, brush-out, nail trim, sanitary trim, lion cuts. Scheduled during quiet hours for a calm experience.

Is my dog safe with you?+

All groomers are Fear Free certified and pet first aid trained. Cage-free drying, gentle handling, one groomer per pet. We never sedate animals.
